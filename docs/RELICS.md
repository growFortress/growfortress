# Relics

Relics are powerful items obtained after each wave. Choose 1 of 3 offered relics.

## Overview

27 relics across 7 categories:

| Category | Count | Description |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Build-Defining | 4 | Shapes entire playstyle |
| Standard | 7 | Basic stat bonuses (incl. physics defense) |
| Class | 5 | Class-specific bonuses |
| Pillar | 3 | Sector-specific bonuses |
| Synergy | 2 | Multi-system bonuses |
| Economy | 3 | Gold/dust bonuses |
| Cursed | 3 | High risk, high reward |

---

## Build-Defining Relics

These relics fundamentally change your build strategy.

### Splash Master
- **Rarity**: Epic
- **Effect**: Attacks deal 35% damage to nearby enemies (+3 splash radius)
- **Synergy**: Konfiguracja Termiczna, Wieża Artyleryjska
- **Build Type**: Splash

### Chain Lightning
- **Rarity**: Epic
- **Effect**: +40% chain chance, +2 chain targets, 60% chain damage
- **Synergy**: Konfiguracja Elektryczna, Wieża Łukowa
- **Build Type**: Chain

### Executioner
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Effect**: Execute enemies below 15% HP for +200% damage (3x total)
- **Synergy**: High attack speed builds
- **Build Type**: Execute

### Glass Cannon
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Effect**: +100% damage, -40% max HP
- **Synergy**: High DPS builds
- **Build Type**: Pure damage

## Relic Selection Strategy

### Early Game (Waves 1-10)
- Prioritize damage relics (Sharpened Blades)
- Iron Hide for survivability
- Avoid cursed relics until you understand the run

### Mid Game (Waves 11-40)
- Look for build-defining relics
- Class relics if you're committed to a configuration
- Economy relics (Gold Rush) for late-game scaling

### Late Game (Waves 41-80)
- Stack synergy relics
- Consider cursed relics for burst potential
- Pillar relics for boss fights
- Elite Hunter for elite-heavy waves

### End Game (Waves 80+)
- Maximize synergy bonuses
- Look for Harmonic Resonance if running full-class team
- Lucky Charm for maximizing drops

---

## Relic Selection System

When offered relics:
1. Relics are filtered by your current class, pillar, and fortress level
2. Already-owned relics are excluded
3. Selection is weighted by rarity
4. 3 relics are offered per wave (unless fewer available)
