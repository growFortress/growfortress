# Mastery System

## Overview

Seven mastery trees (one per class) with 18 nodes each. Maximum 100 mastery points to complete all nodes in a single tree.

### Tree Structure
| Tier | Nodes | Cost Each | Total |
|------|-------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | 3 | 1 MP | 3 MP |
| 2 | 4 | 2 MP | 8 MP |
| 3 | 4 | 4 MP | 16 MP |
| 4 | 4 | 6 MP | 24 MP |
| 5 | 3 | 10 MP | 30 MP |
| **Total** | **18** | - | **81 MP** |

### Node Types
- **stat_bonus**: Passive stat increase
- **synergy_amplifier**: Synergy bonus multiplier
- **class_perk**: Unique class ability
- **capstone**: Tier 5 ultimate ability

## Synergy Node Pattern

Each tree has 4 synergy-focused nodes:

| Tier | Node | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| 2 | Hero Synergy | +15% hero synergy bonus |
| 2 | Turret Synergy | +15% turret synergy bonus |
| 3 | Full Synergy | +25% full synergy bonus |
| 4 | Synergy Mastery | All synergy +20% |

**Unity Capstones** (all Tier 5):
- Reduced requirements: 1 hero + 2 turrets (instead of 2H + 3T)
- +50% synergy bonus
